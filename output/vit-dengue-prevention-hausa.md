## 3. Source text (English) — for reviewer comparison only

*Reconstructed from the WHO "Dengue and severe dengue" fact sheet — VERIFY VERBATIM (see Provenance note).*

### Key facts
- Dengue is a viral infection spread to people through the bite of infected mosquitoes.
- About half of the world's population is now at risk of dengue, with an estimated 100–400 million infections each year.
- Dengue is found in tropical and sub-tropical climates, mostly in urban and semi-urban areas.
- Many dengue infections cause no symptoms or only mild illness, but the virus can sometimes cause more severe illness and even death.
- There is no specific treatment for dengue. Prevention and control depend on controlling the mosquitoes that spread it. Early detection and proper medical care greatly lower the risk of death from severe dengue.

### Overview
Dengue is caused by a virus (DENV) spread by the bite of infected female mosquitoes, mainly the *Aedes aegypti* mosquito and, to a lesser extent, *Aedes albopictus*. These mosquitoes also spread chikungunya, yellow fever and Zika. There are four serotypes of the dengue virus (DENV-1 to DENV-4), so it is possible to be infected more than once. Recovery from infection by one serotype gives lifelong immunity against only that serotype; a later infection by a different serotype increases the risk of developing severe dengue.

### Signs and symptoms
Most people with dengue have no symptoms or only mild symptoms and get better in 1–2 weeks. Symptoms usually start 4–10 days after the mosquito bite and last 2–7 days. They can include:
- high fever (40 °C / 104 °F)
- severe headache
- pain behind the eyes
- muscle and joint pains
- nausea and vomiting
- swollen glands
- rash

**Severe dengue — danger signs.** A person with dengue can suddenly become seriously ill, often **after the fever has gone away**. Get medical care immediately if any of these appear:
- severe abdominal (belly) pain
- persistent vomiting
- rapid breathing
- bleeding gums or nose
- blood in vomit or stool
- great tiredness, restlessness or weakness
- feeling very thirsty; pale and cold skin

Severe dengue is a medical emergency. Without proper care it can be fatal.

### Treatment and self-care
There is no specific medicine to treat dengue. Most people with mild dengue can be cared for at home. If you have dengue:
- rest;
- drink plenty of fluids to stay hydrated;
- use **paracetamol (acetaminophen)** to control fever and pain;
- **avoid non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) such as ibuprofen and aspirin**, because they can increase the risk of bleeding;
- watch for the danger signs above and go to a doctor or health facility immediately if any appear.

People with severe dengue often need care in a hospital.

### Vaccination
A dengue vaccine exists and may be offered in some settings as part of an integrated prevention approach, but vaccination **does not replace** mosquito-bite protection and mosquito control. Follow the guidance of your national health authority on who should be vaccinated.

### Prevention and control
The mosquitoes that spread dengue **bite mainly during the day**. There is no specific treatment, so the best protection is to avoid mosquito bites and to reduce the number of mosquitoes.

**Protect yourself from mosquito bites:**
- wear clothing that covers as much of the body as possible;
- use mosquito nets while sleeping during the day, ideally nets treated with insecticide;
- use window and door screens;
- use mosquito repellents containing **DEET, Picaridin or IR3535**, following the label instructions;
- use mosquito coils and vaporizers.

**Stop mosquitoes from breeding** (remove the standing water where they lay eggs):
- cover, empty and clean household water-storage containers **every week**;
- remove or properly dispose of solid waste and any items that can hold water (old tyres, tins, pots, buckets);
- where appropriate, apply suitable insecticides to outdoor water-storage containers.

**Community action.** Protecting a whole community works better than acting alone. Support community clean-up campaigns and any official mosquito-control or space-spraying activities during outbreaks.

## 4. Hausa translation / Fassara cikin Hausa

*Fassarar WHO "Dengue and severe dengue" fact sheet. Asalin rubutu na Turanci ne ke da inganci da iko (binding); duba sashe na 8 don sanarwar fassara.*

### Muhimman bayanai
- Zazzaɓin **dengue** cuta ce da ke yaɗuwa zuwa ga mutane ta hanyar cizon **sauro** masu ɗauke da ƙwayar cutar.
- A yanzu kusan rabin mutanen duniya na cikin haɗarin kamuwa da dengue, kuma ana ƙiyasin kamuwa miliyan 100 zuwa 400 a kowace shekara.
- Ana samun dengue a yankunan da ke da zafi (tropical da sub-tropical), galibi a birane da kewayen birane.
- Yawancin kamuwa da dengue ba ya nuna alama ko yana haifar da rashin lafiya mai sauƙi kawai, amma a wasu lokuta ƙwayar cutar na iya haifar da rashin lafiya mai tsanani har ma da mutuwa.
- **Babu takamaiman magani** na dengue. Rigakafi da shawo kan cutar sun dogara ne kan shawo kan sauro masu yaɗa ta. Gano cutar da wuri da samun kulawar likita yadda ya kamata na rage haɗarin mutuwa daga dengue mai tsanani sosai.

### Bayani gaba ɗaya
Zazzaɓin dengue na faruwa ne sakamakon wata ƙwayar cuta (**DENV**) da ke yaɗuwa ta cizon sauro mata masu ɗauke da ita, musamman sauron **_Aedes aegypti_** sannan kuma, a ƙarami, **_Aedes albopictus_**. Waɗannan sauro suna kuma yaɗa chikungunya, da yellow fever (zazzaɓin shawara), da Zika. Akwai nau'o'i huɗu na ƙwayar cutar dengue (**DENV-1 zuwa DENV-4**), saboda haka mutum na iya kamuwa fiye da sau ɗaya. Warkewa daga wani nau'i guda yana ba da kāriya ta dindindin daga **wannan nau'i kaɗai**; kamuwa daga baya da wani nau'i daban na ƙara haɗarin samun dengue mai tsanani.

### Alamomin cutar
Yawancin masu dengue ba sa nuna wata alama ko suna da alamomi masu sauƙi kawai, kuma suna warkewa cikin mako ɗaya zuwa biyu. Alamomin yawanci na fara bayyana kwana 4 zuwa 10 bayan cizon sauro, kuma suna ɗaukar kwana 2 zuwa 7. Suna iya haɗawa da:
- zazzaɓi mai tsanani (40 °C / 104 °F)
- ciwon kai mai tsanani
- ciwo a bayan idanu
- ciwon tsokoki da gaɓɓai (muscle da joint pain)
- tashin zuciya da amai
- kumburin glandar (swollen glands)
- ƙuraje a fata (rash)

**Dengue mai tsanani — alamomin haɗari.** Mai dengue na iya kamuwa da rashin lafiya mai tsanani ba zato ba tsammani, galibi **bayan zazzaɓin ya sauka**. Nemi kulawar likita **nan da nan** idan ɗaya daga cikin waɗannan ya bayyana:
- ciwon ciki mai tsanani
- amai mai dorewa (wanda ba ya tsayawa)
- numfashi mai sauri
- zubar jini daga ɗasashi (gums) ko hanci
- jini a cikin amai ko bayan gari (stool)
- gajiya mai yawa, rashin nutsuwa, ko rauni
- jin ƙishirwa mai yawa; fata mai kodewa da sanyi

Dengue mai tsanani gaggawar lafiya ce. Idan ba a kula da ita yadda ya kamata ba, tana iya kashe mutum.

### Magani da kulawar kai
**Babu takamaiman magani** na dengue. Yawancin masu dengue mai sauƙi ana iya kula da su a gida. Idan kana da dengue:
- ka huta;
- ka sha ruwa mai yawa don kada jiki ya bushe (don kāriya daga rashin ruwa a jiki);
- yi amfani da **paracetamol (acetaminophen)** don rage zazzaɓi da ciwo;
- **ka guji magungunan rage kumburi marasa steroid (NSAIDs) kamar ibuprofen da aspirin**, domin suna iya ƙara haɗarin zubar jini;
- ka kula da alamomin haɗari da ke sama, kuma **ka je wurin likita ko cibiyar lafiya nan da nan** idan ɗayansu ya bayyana.

Masu dengue mai tsanani sukan buƙaci kulawa a asibiti.

### Allurar rigakafi (vaccine)
Akwai allurar rigakafin dengue (**dengue vaccine**), kuma ana iya bayar da ita a wasu wurare a matsayin wani ɓangare na hanyar rigakafi mai haɗaka, amma allurar rigakafi **ba ta maye gurbin** kāriya daga cizon sauro da shawo kan sauro ba. Bi shawarar hukumar lafiya ta ƙasarka kan wa ya kamata a yi wa allurar.

### Rigakafi da shawo kan cutar
Sauro masu yaɗa dengue **suna cizo galibi da rana**. Tunda babu takamaiman magani, kāriya mafi kyau ita ce **kauce wa cizon sauro** da **rage yawan sauro**.

**Ka kāre kanka daga cizon sauro:**
- sanya tufafi da ke rufe jiki gwargwadon yiwuwa;
- yi amfani da **gidan sauro** (mosquito net) idan kana barci da rana, mafi kyau wanda aka shafa wa maganin kashe ƙwari;
- yi amfani da raga a tagogi da ƙofofi (window/door screens);
- yi amfani da maganin korar sauro (mosquito repellent) mai ɗauke da **DEET, Picaridin ko IR3535**, bisa ga umarnin da ke kan kwali;
- yi amfani da turaren sauro (coils) da na'urar fitar da hayaƙin sauro (vaporizers).

**Ka hana sauro haihuwa** (kawar da tsayayyen ruwa inda suke yin ƙwai):
- rufe, zuba, da wanke kwanonin ajiye ruwa na gida **a kowane mako**;
- kawar da shara da kowane abu da zai iya riƙe ruwa yadda ya kamata (tayoyi tsofaffi, gwangwani, tukwane, bokiti);
- inda ya dace, sa magungunan kashe ƙwari masu dacewa a kwanonin ajiye ruwa na waje.

**Aikin al'umma.** Kāre dukan al'umma yana aiki fiye da yin aiki kai kaɗai. Ka goyi bayan yaƙin neman tsaftace muhalli na al'umma da duk wani aikin shawo kan sauro ko fesa magani da hukuma ke yi a lokacin ɓarkewar cutar.
