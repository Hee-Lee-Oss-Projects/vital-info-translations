**Hausa:** Wannan takarda na ɗauke da bayani kan rigakafin da ya kamata yaro ya samu tun daga
haihuwa har zuwa balaga. An rubuta ta cikin Hausa da Turanci gefe-da-gefe domin a sami sauƙin

> *English: This document lists the vaccines a child should receive from birth to adolescence. It
> is laid out in Hausa and English side by side for ease of checking. The technical vaccine and
> disease names kept in English (e.g. **BCG**, **OPV**, **Pentavalent**) are preserved exactly so

## 3. Me ya sa rigakafi ke da muhimmanci (Why vaccination matters)

**Hausa:**
- Rigakafi yana **kare yaro daga cututtuka masu-haɗari** waɗanda za su iya kashe shi ko su naƙasa shi.
- Yawancin waɗannan cututtukan **ba su da magani mai sauƙi** idan sun kama yaro — **rigakafi ya fi sauƙi kuma ya fi araha** fiye da jiyya.
- Idan yara da yawa suka sami rigakafi, cutar ba ta yaɗuwa cikin al'umma — wannan na kare ko da yaran da ba a iya yi musu rigakafi ba.
- Rigakafin yara **lafiyayye ne** kuma galibi ana ba shi **kyauta** a asibitocin gwamnati.

> *English: Vaccines protect children from dangerous diseases that can kill or disable them. Most
> of these diseases are hard to treat once a child catches them — vaccination is easier and
> cheaper than treatment. When most children are vaccinated, the disease cannot spread easily,
> which also protects children who cannot be vaccinated. Childhood vaccines are safe and are
> usually given free of charge at government health facilities.*

## 4. Jadawalin Rigakafi bisa Shekarun Yaro (Vaccination schedule by age)

> Sunayen rigakafi da na cututtuka an ajiye su a Turanci (an kiyaye kalmomin likitanci). Bayanin
> cututtukan da kowane rigakafi ke karewa yana cikin **Sashe na 5 (ƙamus)**.
> *Vaccine/disease names are kept in English (medical terms preserved). What each vaccine protects
> against is explained in the **glossary, Section 5**.*

| Shekaru / Age | Rigakafi / Vaccines (an kiyaye sunaye) | Yadda ake bayarwa / How given |
|---|---|---|
| **Da haihuwa** — *At birth* | **BCG**; **OPV-0** (kashin haihuwa); **Hepatitis B** (kashin haihuwa, cikin sa'o'i 24) | BCG: allura a hannun dama na sama; OPV: digo a baki; HepB: allura |
| **Makonni 6** — *6 weeks* | **Pentavalent** (DTP-HepB-Hib) 1; **OPV-1**; **PCV** 1; **Rotavirus** 1 | Allura (Penta, PCV); digo a baki (OPV, Rotavirus) |
| **Makonni 10** — *10 weeks* | **Pentavalent** 2; **OPV-2**; **PCV** 2; **Rotavirus** 2 | Allura da digo a baki |
| **Makonni 14** — *14 weeks* | **Pentavalent** 3; **OPV-3**; **PCV** 3; **Rotavirus** 3¹; **IPV** 1 | Allura da digo a baki |
| **Watanni 9** — *9 months* | **Measles** (ko **MR**, Measles-Rubella) 1; **Yellow Fever**²; **MenA**³; **Vitamin A** | Allura; Vitamin A: ɗigo/kpasula a baki |
| **Watanni 12–15** — *12–15 months* | **Measles** (ko **MR**) 2 | Allura |
| **Watanni 15–18** — *15–18 months*⁴ | **DTP**-containing booster (kashi na ƙarawa) | Allura |
| **'Yan mata shekaru 9–14** — *Girls 9–14 yrs* | **HPV** (kashi 1–2) | Allura |

**Bayanin tauraro / Footnotes:**
1. **Rotavirus**: kashi 2 ko 3 ya danganta da nau'in maganin da ake amfani da shi. *2 or 3 doses depending on the product used.*
2. **Yellow Fever**: a yankunan da cutar ke yawa kawai (yawancin Najeriya na cikin haka). *Only in yellow-fever-endemic areas (most of Nigeria qualifies).*
3. **MenA** (rigakafin sankarau): a yankin "meningitis belt" na Afirka (ya haɗa da arewacin Najeriya da Nijar). *In the African meningitis belt, which includes northern Nigeria and Niger.*
4. **IPV** da kashin ƙarawa na **DTP**: adadi da lokaci na iya bambanta — **a bi jadawalin ƙasa**. *Number/timing of IPV and DTP boosters may vary — follow the national schedule.*

> **Rigakafin zazzaɓin cizon sauro (Malaria) / Malaria vaccine:** A yankunan da zazzaɓin cizon
> sauro ke yawa, WHO ya ba da shawarar rigakafin malaria (**RTS,S** ko **R21**) ga yara tun daga
> kusan watanni 5 (kashi 4–5). **A bi shirin rigakafi na ƙasa** kan ko ana bayar da shi da yadda
> ake ba shi a wurinku.
> *In malaria-endemic areas WHO recommends the malaria vaccine (RTS,S or R21) for children from
> about 5 months (4–5 doses). Follow the national programme on availability and dosing.*

## 6. Shawarwari masu Amfani ga Iyaye da Ma'aikatan Lafiya (Practical guidance)

**Hausa:**
1. **A kawo katin rigakafi** (katin yaro) a kowace ziyara, kuma a ajiye shi lafiya. *Bring the immunization card to every visit and keep it safe.*
2. **A kammala dukkan kashi a kan lokaci.** Idan an makara, **ba a daina ba** — a kawo yaro a ci gaba daga inda aka tsaya. *Complete all doses on time. If late, do not give up — resume from where you stopped.*
3. **Ƙananan illoli na al'ada ne:** zazzaɓi kaɗan, kumburi ko ja a wurin allura, ko juyayi na kwana ɗaya ko biyu. Yawanci suna wucewa da kansu. *Mild side effects — slight fever, swelling/redness at the injection site, fussiness for a day or two — are normal and usually pass on their own.*
4. Don zazzaɓi ko ciwo, a ba yaro **ruwa mai yawa** kuma a sanyaya shi; idan ya cancanta, a yi amfani da maganin zafi yadda ma'aikacin lafiya ya umarta. *For fever/pain, give plenty of fluids and keep the child cool; use a fever medicine only as advised by a health worker.*
5. **Yaron da ke da ƙaramar rashin lafiya (kamar mura ko ɗan zazzaɓi) ana iya yi masa rigakafi.** Kada a jinkirta sai ka tambayi ma'aikacin lafiya. *A child with a mild illness (e.g. a cold or low fever) can still be vaccinated — ask the health worker before postponing.*
6. **A je asibiti nan da nan** idan bayan rigakafi yaro ya yi: zazzaɓi mai-tsanani da bai sauka ba, ciwon da ya wuce kwana biyu, wahalar numfashi, kumburin fuska ko makogwaro, ko juyayi/suma. *Seek care immediately if after vaccination the child has: high fever that does not settle, crying/pain lasting more than two days, difficulty breathing, swelling of the face or throat, or convulsions/unresponsiveness.*
7. Idan an rasa wani kashi ko ba a san matsayin yaro ba, **a tuntuɓi ma'aikacin lafiya** don tsara yadda za a cike gibin. *If a dose was missed or the child's status is unknown, contact a health worker to plan catch-up.*
