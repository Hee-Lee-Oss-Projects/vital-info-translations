### 3.1 Utangulizi / Introduction

**(Kiswahili)**
Chanjo humkinga mtoto dhidi ya magonjwa hatari yanayoweza kuzuilika. Ili kinga iwe kamili, ni muhimu
mtoto apate **kila dozi (dose) kwa wakati uliopangwa** na **kukamilisha dozi zote**. Ratiba iliyo hapa
chini inategemea mapendekezo ya **Shirika la Afya Ulimwenguni (WHO)** kwa chanjo za kawaida za watoto.
**Nchi mbalimbali zina ratiba zao rasmi za kitaifa (Wizara ya Afya / Programu ya Kitaifa ya Chanjo —
EPI)** ambazo zinaweza kutofautiana kidogo. **Daima fuata ratiba rasmi ya nchi yako.** Kama mtoto
amekosa dozi, mpeleke kituo cha afya haraka iwezekanavyo — si lazima kuanza upya; mhudumu wa afya
atakamilisha dozi zilizokosa.

**(English source)**
Vaccines protect a child against dangerous, preventable diseases. For full protection a child must
receive **each dose on schedule** and **complete all doses**. The schedule below is based on **World
Health Organization (WHO)** recommendations for routine childhood immunization. **Individual countries
have their own official national schedules (Ministry of Health / national Expanded Programme on
Immunization — EPI)** which may differ slightly. **Always follow your country's official schedule.**
If a child misses a dose, take them to a health facility as soon as possible — there is usually no need
to restart; the health worker will complete the missed doses.

### 3.2 Jedwali la ratiba / Schedule table

> ⚠️ **Reviewer: verify every age, dose count and interval against the live WHO summary table and the
> relevant national EPI schedule before sign-off.** Values reflect the long-standing WHO routine
> schedule but were not fetched live in this session.

| Umri / Age | Chanjo (Kiswahili) | Vaccine (English) | Hulinda dhidi ya / Protects against |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Wakati wa kuzaliwa / At birth** | BCG | BCG | Kifua kikuu / Tuberculosis (severe forms) |
| **Wakati wa kuzaliwa / At birth** | Homa ya ini aina ya B — dozi ya kuzaliwa (ndani ya saa 24) | Hepatitis B (birth dose, within 24 h) | Homa ya ini / Hepatitis B |
| **Wakati wa kuzaliwa / At birth** | Polio kwa njia ya mdomo — dozi ya kuzaliwa (OPV-0, pale inapotumika) | Oral polio (OPV-0, where used) | Polio / Poliomyelitis |
| **Wiki 6 / 6 weeks** | Polio (OPV-1); Chanjo mseto ya dondakoo–pepopunda–kifaduro–homa ya ini B–Hib (Pentavalent, dozi ya 1); Nimonia (PCV-1); Rotavirus (dozi ya 1) | OPV-1; Pentavalent-1 (DTP–HepB–Hib); PCV-1; Rotavirus-1 | Polio; dondakoo (diphtheria), pepopunda (tetanus), kifaduro (pertussis/whooping cough), homa ya ini B, Hib; nimonia/pneumococcal; rotavirus |
| **Wiki 10 / 10 weeks** | Polio (OPV-2); Pentavalent (dozi ya 2); Nimonia (PCV-2); Rotavirus (dozi ya 2) | OPV-2; Pentavalent-2; PCV-2; Rotavirus-2 | Kama ilivyo hapo juu / As above |
| **Wiki 14 / 14 weeks** | Polio (OPV-3); Polio ya sindano (IPV); Pentavalent (dozi ya 3); Nimonia (PCV-3); Rotavirus (dozi ya 3, kama chanjo inahitaji dozi 3) | OPV-3; IPV (injectable polio); Pentavalent-3; PCV-3; Rotavirus-3 (if 3-dose product) | Kama ilivyo hapo juu / As above |
| **Miezi 9 / 9 months** | Surua–Rubela (dozi ya 1) | Measles–Rubella (MR-1) | Surua (measles), rubela (rubella) |
| **Miezi 9 / 9 months (nchi zenye hatari)** | Homa ya manjano (pale inapohitajika) | Yellow fever (in endemic countries) | Homa ya manjano / Yellow fever |
| **Miezi 15–18 / 15–18 months** | Surua–Rubela (dozi ya 2) | Measles–Rubella (MR-2) | Kama ilivyo hapo juu / As above |
| **Miaka 9–14 (wasichana) / 9–14 years (girls)** | HPV (dozi 1–2 kulingana na mwongozo) | HPV (1–2 doses per guidance) | Saratani ya shingo ya kizazi / Cervical cancer (HPV) |

**Vidokezo / Key notes:**

- **(KSW)** Muda wa chini kati ya dozi za msingi ni kawaida **wiki 4**. Boosters/nyongeza zinaweza
  kutolewa baadaye kulingana na ratiba ya kitaifa. Idadi ya dozi za rotavirus (2 au 3) na za polio ya
  sindano (IPV) hutegemea aina ya chanjo na mwongozo wa sasa wa WHO/kitaifa.
  **(ENG)** Minimum interval between primary doses is usually **4 weeks**. Boosters may be given later
  per the national schedule. The number of rotavirus doses (2 or 3) and of injectable polio (IPV)
  doses depends on the product and current WHO/national guidance.

- **(KSW)** Chanjo za ziada (kwa mfano homa ya manjano, homa ya uti wa mgongo — meningococcal,
  Japanese encephalitis, homa ya matumbo — typhoid) hupendekezwa **kulingana na hali ya ugonjwa katika
  eneo husika**. Fuata mwongozo wa kitaifa.
  **(ENG)** Additional vaccines (e.g. yellow fever, meningococcal, Japanese encephalitis, typhoid) are
  recommended **according to local disease epidemiology**. Follow national guidance.

- **(KSW)** Watoto waliozaliwa kabla ya wakati (njiti) au wenye magonjwa wanaweza kuhitaji ushauri wa
  ziada — muulize mhudumu wa afya.
  **(ENG)** Preterm or sick children may need additional advice — ask a health worker.
